Project S is a groundbreaking game and tournament management platform designed specifically for Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 tournaments, with the aim of revolutionising the gaming experience for players. The platform's objective is to simplify team management, streamline matchmaking, facilitate map decision-making, enable score uploads, and incorporate AI-enabled reporting features to identify and address hardscope kills, enhancing fairness and competitiveness.

The platform features a streamlined team registration process with immediate confirmations, skill-based matchmaking for balanced encounters, interactive map selection through a rock-paper-scissors showdown, transparent score uploads, and a robust reporting system powered by AI for incident evaluation. With the use of React for the front end and Django for the backend, our team meticulously engineered Project S to seamlessly blend robust functionality with user-friendly design, marking a paradigm shift in gaming. As SeedInov, we take pride in contributing to this transformative journey, offering a tool that upholds the essence of Project S and promotes innovation in the gaming landscape.
