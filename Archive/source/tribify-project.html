Client Objective: Enhancing Workplace Wellbeing with Tribify

Our clients envisioned a tool that not only manages resources but also genuinely cares for employees' emotional and psychological well-being. Tribify was conceptualized to provide a holistic approach to HRM by regularly surveying employees to gauge their emotions and generating wellness scores based on their responses. The aim was to maintain a pulse on the workplace atmosphere and promote a supportive environment.

Our Engagement: Building a Comprehensive Wellbeing Assistant

Our team partnered with the client to develop Tribify from the ground up. We delivered end-to-end services, including SaaS development and MVP creation, to ensure that Tribify met all the project requirements. Our involvement was pivotal in turning the client's vision into a tangible product that supports and enhances employee well-being.

The Product: Tribify, A Comprehensive HRM and Wellbeing Solution

Tribify stands out with its unique features such as regular employee surveys that track emotional well-being and generate wellness scores. The tool also celebrates important milestones and events, like birthdays and work anniversaries, fostering a culture of celebration. Additionally, Tribify includes all the essential HRM functionalities required for effective resource management, making it a holistic solution for modern workplaces.
